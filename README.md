# attic-protocol
Decentralized anonymity layer based on SMPC and Ephemeral Sharding.
Attic Protocol 🏺
Privacy through fragmentation, not obfuscation.
Attic is a next-generation privacy protocol that breaks transactions into mathematical shards using Secure Multi-Party Computation (SMPC).

Key Features:
No ZKP Overhead: Fast processing on any device.

Ephemeral Shards: Links are broken during the "Distillation" phase.

Quantum Resistant: Based on information-theoretic security.

**Author:** Petr Blinov  
**Status:** Research & Development (Phase 1: Fragmentation)

🏛 Philosophy: The Right to Mathematics
In a world of total surveillance, privacy is often treated as a luxury or a suspicion. We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right, guaranteed by the laws of mathematics.
Integrity without transparency: We prove the truth without exposing the data.
Sovereignty: You own your fragments; no single entity owns the whole.
Resilience: Attic Protocol is built to survive in a post-quantum world.
